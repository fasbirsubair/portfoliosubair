Smart Helmet

Our project integrates a robust safety system for motorcycles that enhances road safety by ensuring helmet use and preventing drunk driving. It uses computer vision to verify helmet presence before starting the bike's engine. Additionally, it continuously monitors alcohol levels; if detected, it initiates engine shutdown to prevent accidents, both during operation and before starting the engine. This system promotes legal compliance and significantly reduces the risk of accidents, making roads safer for riders and pedestrians alike.
